What Is Sushi Sushi?

Sushi Sushi is a new restaruant chain based in Washington D.C. by Japanese-American entrepreneur Sakura Iku. Her plan is to bring an exciting, reinvented experience to what Americans view now as sushi. From hands-on classes in restaurants about the basics of how it's made, to thorough, engaging discussions from experts on the history & defintions, Sushi Sushi is going to change the way you see Japanese cuisine.
